PNG Background Remover

Drop one or more PNGs below. The background color is auto-detected from each image's own corner (whatever color it is), then removed via edge-connected flood fill with a smooth, halo-free edge - works on flat/solid backgrounds (logos, icons, badges), not on photos or gradients. Nothing is uploaded anywhere; everything runs locally in this page.

Lower = stricter background match. Raise if background pixels are left behind; lower if it's eating into the artwork.
